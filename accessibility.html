Accessibility

I want as many people as possible to be able to use my page. I decided to follow the Worldwide Web Consortium guidelines and the UK government guidelines considering accessibility.

My page was designed with mobile and touchscreen in mind. It contains large clickable elements, readable font and good use of colour constrast between text and background. Zoom was ensured to be enabled. I also included alt property for image elements considering screen readers. Colour pallete used in the page has been chosen having in mind different types of colour blindness. At least one colour pallete in the graphs is accessible for any of the most common types.
